ACP exam format — what to expect
Section 1 — Selected Response

Multiple-choice and scenario-based questions covering Domain 1 concepts: design terminology, copyright, colour theory, and design principles. No live software required.

Section 2 — Live-in-the-App

Task-based questions performed directly inside Photoshop. You receive a brief and must complete specific tasks using the real application. This is what the simulator prepares you for.

Exam duration: 50 minutes  ·  Target candidate: ~150 hours of instruction and hands-on experience  ·  Delivered by: Certiport / NCS Pearson
