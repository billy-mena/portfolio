Kirkpatrick Model — ACP Photoshop 2025

A four-level evaluation framework measuring the impact of this training. This toolkit covers Levels 1–3, which are measurable within the course context. Level 4 (Results) requires longitudinal tracking beyond course completion.

L1
Reaction — Did learners find it valuable?
Measures learner satisfaction, perceived relevance, and engagement. Administered immediately after course completion.
5 Likert items (1–5 scale) · 2 open-ended questions · ~3 minutes to complete
L2
Learning — Did learners gain knowledge and skills?
Measures knowledge acquisition through matched pre/post assessments. Compares scores before and after instruction to quantify learning gain.
5 pre-course items + 5 matched post-course items · Aligned to ACP exam objectives · ~8 minutes
L3
Behavior — Are learners applying skills on the job?
Measures observable behavior transfer to the workplace or practice context. Uses direct observation and supervisor/instructor interviews conducted 2–4 weeks after course completion.
10-item behavioral observation checklist · 5-question supervisor/instructor interview guide
