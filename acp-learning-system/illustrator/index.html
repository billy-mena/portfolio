Course info
ACP Illustrator exam format
50-minute exam, two sections: multiple-choice selected-response items (Domain 1 concepts) followed by Live-in-the-App tasks inside real Illustrator (Domains 2–5).
  • MCDesign industry concepts, terminology, copyright — no software needed
  • LiveTask-based: perform actions directly in Illustrator
  • Target~150 hours of instruction and hands-on experience
  • New '25AI objective: Generate Vectors (Ai-4.1e) — testable in 2025
